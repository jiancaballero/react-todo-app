How to play

Goal:Remove all cards on the deck before you run out of life

There are four types of cards:

  • Buhay Cards(Life Cards)
  • Lakas Cards(Energy Cards)
  • Barya Cards(Coin Cards)
  • Kamatayan Cards(Life Damage Cards)

Point Indicators

At the top-left of the screen are the point indicators

There are four indicators:

  • Buhay Points
  • Lakas Points
  • Barya Points
  • Silip Points

Buhay(Life Points)

This indicates your total life points

The maximum cap is 10. The game is over once it drops to 0

You can gain buhay from Buhay Cards

Lakas (Energy Points)

This indicates your total energy points

The maximum cap is 10

These points allow you to reveal and select a card.

If you ran out of energy points, your life points will be used to a reveal a card.

You can gain lakas from Lakas Cards

Barya (Coin Points)

This indicates your total coin points

There is no maximum cap.

It allows you to buy a silip (Peek)

You can gain barya from Barya Cards

Silip(Peek Points)

This indicates your total silip points

You can only have a max of 1 silip

Silip (Peek) can be bought for 5 coins

You can use silip to take a peek at the Kamatayan Cards (Life Damage Cards)

Once used, the next purchase of silip is available after the second selection of cards.

Card Types

Buhay Cards

Buhay cards are represented by the red color cards

These cards increase your life points depending on the given value of the card

Buhay Cards will not increase life points when you reach the max cap

Lakas Cards

Lakas cards are represented by the green color cards

These cards increase your energy points depending on the given value of the card

Lakas Cards will not increase energy points when you reach the max cap

Barya Cards

Barya cards are represented by the yellow color cards

These cards increase your coin points depending on the given value of the card

Kamatayan Cards

Kamatayan Cards are represented by the purple cards

These cards decrease your life points depending on the given value of the card

You can take a peek on these cards when you buy and use your silip points
